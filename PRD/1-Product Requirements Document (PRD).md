# AFAB - Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Executive Summary

### Product Name

**AFAB (AI Finance Assistant for Business)**

### Product Type

Cloud-based Software as a Service (SaaS)

### Version

MVP (Version 1.0)

### Overview

AFAB (AI Finance Assistant for Business) is a modern SaaS platform designed to help small businesses manage their finances through a clean, intuitive, and data-driven experience. The platform enables business owners to monitor income, expenses, budgets, savings goals, investments, and financial performance from a single dashboard.

The initial release (MVP) focuses on providing a complete finance management solution without artificial intelligence. However, the application is designed with a scalable architecture that allows seamless integration of AI-powered features in future versions.

AFAB emphasizes simplicity, usability, and performance. Rather than competing with complex enterprise accounting systems, it targets businesses that need an accessible and modern financial management tool to improve financial awareness and decision-making.

### Purpose

The purpose of AFAB is to simplify financial management for small businesses by providing a centralized platform where users can organize financial data, monitor business performance, and make informed financial decisions.

### Product Vision

To become the intelligent financial operating system for small businesses by combining intuitive financial management with future AI-driven insights.

### Product Mission

To empower business owners with a secure, scalable, and user-friendly platform that simplifies financial management while preparing them for the next generation of AI-assisted business intelligence.

### MVP Objective

The first version of AFAB will provide essential financial management capabilities including:

* User authentication and account management
* Financial dashboard
* Income management
* Expense management
* Budget planning
* Savings goals
* Manual investment tracking
* Financial reports
* Notifications
* User settings

AI-powered features such as financial recommendations, predictive analytics, conversational assistants, and automated categorization are intentionally excluded from Version 1 and planned for future releases.

### Long-Term Vision

AFAB is intended to evolve into a comprehensive business finance platform capable of assisting users through intelligent automation, predictive financial analysis, natural language interaction, and personalized business insights while maintaining a premium user experience and scalable architecture.
